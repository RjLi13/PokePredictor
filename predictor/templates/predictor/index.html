PokePredictor

Simple webapp to calculate which move you should choose through expected values of possible moves in pokemon showdown. As of now, it only applies for 5th gen (BW). We do not take credit for Pokemon, and we like to thank PokeApi, Pykemon and Honko Damage Calculator in Showdown for Use of their pokemon database and inspiration.
